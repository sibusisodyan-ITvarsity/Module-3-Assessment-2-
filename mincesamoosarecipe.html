Instructions

  1. Put 2 tbsp oil in frying pan, add mince and cook till all the water dries up.
  2. Add salt and red chilies to the mince and fry. Add coriander leaves, crushed cumin seeds, chilies, and onion.
  3. Increase the flame and thoroughly mix all the spices, fry and turn off the flame.
  4. Fill the manga strips with mince mixture and shape them like samosas. Seal the ends with flour and water paste.
  5. Heat oil in the wok and fry the samosas till they are golden brown. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.

Enjoy your home cooked meal!
