Milk Tart

Melktert(Milk tart) originated from the Dutch settlers who arrived in Cape Town, South Africa, in the 1600s. Melktert is Afrikaans for milk tart and is a dessert which has a sweet pastry crust and a creamy custard filling made from eggs, flour, milk and sugar
